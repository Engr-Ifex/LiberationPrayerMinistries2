Pastor Modele Fatoyinbo, Co-Senior Pastor of COZA, is passionate about drawing people into a closer walk with God. She shares her husbandu2019s passion for National Transformation and loving Godu2019s people.

Her desire to see women rise up to be who they were designed to be propelled her to found the COZA Lady Network that produces several events and conferences that have transformed lives across the country.

Pastor Modele is a model and mentor to women who want to fulfil their destinies while keeping their families intact. She believes in the importance of a truly balanced life and models the same by being the affectionate, supportive wife and loving mother that she is.
